# FinalProject165 Title: Public vs Private Healthcare Purpose: With an ongoing debate about weather healthcare should be controlled privately or by a public sector in the United States, my main goal for this project is to visually represent both sides of the debate. It is extreamly common in this day and age that people only see the side they want to see, yet with this visualzation both sides will be equally represented. This will be a helpful tool to facitile the debate by providing one to one comparisons with the same metrics. I hope to not only educate people about this ongoing debate but also provide some important statistics that could help to facilite a positive change.
